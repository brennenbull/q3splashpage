About Our App

DRINK WATER is a mobile application that aims to solve a simple but important issue - staying hydrated. Our application allows the user to log their daily water intake. Water intake is tracked with past water consumption and compared to daily recomendations.

Why Water?

Your body depends on water to survive. Every cell, organ and tissue requires water to function properly. The body uses water to lubricate joints, maintain its temperature and remove waste. Staying hydrated helps the heart pump blood easier through your blood vessels and to the muscles throughout your body.

What causes overhydration?

What causes overhydration? Overhydration is an imbalance of fluids. It happens when your body takes in or holds on to more fluid than your kidneys can remove.

Benefits of Water

Water plays an important role in regulating temperature flushing out waste products, and helps perform all major bodily functions
